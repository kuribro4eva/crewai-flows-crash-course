With a spark in her eye and a smile so bright, Klee dances through Teyvat with explosive delight, leaving foes in her wake with her Jumpy Dumpty charm and astral precision so tight. Her bullets burst like fireflies in a whimsical show, a symphony of sparks that illuminate the battleground below. Spark Knight of Mondstadt, little troublemaker supreme, Klee's adventures are a joyous, thrilling dream.
